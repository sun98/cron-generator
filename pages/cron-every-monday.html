Cron Job Every Monday

Expression: 0 9 * * 1

Understanding Monday Cron

In cron, days of week are numbered 0-6 where 0 = Sunday and 1 = Monday.

  • 0 9 * * 1 - At 9 AM every Monday
  • 0 0 * * 1 - At midnight every Monday
  • 0 18 * * 1 - At 6 PM every Monday
