Run Cron Every Minute

To run a job every minute, use: * * * * *

Explanation

  • First * = every minute (0-59)
  • Second * = every hour (0-23)
  • Third * = every day of month (1-31)
  • Fourth * = every month (1-12)
  • Fifth * = every day of week (0-6)

Common Variations

  • */5 * * * * - Every 5 minutes
  • */10 * * * * - Every 10 minutes
  • */15 * * * * - Every 15 minutes
