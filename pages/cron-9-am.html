What Time is 9 AM?

In 24-hour format, 9 AM is 9. The expression 0 9 * * * means:

  • 0 - At minute 0
  • 9 - At hour 9 (9 AM)
  • * - Every day of month
  • * - Every month
  • * - Every day of week
