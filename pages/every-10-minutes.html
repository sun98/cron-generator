Cron Every 10 Minutes

Expression: */10 * * * *

How */10 Works

The */10 runs at minutes: 0, 10, 20, 30, 40, and 50 of every hour.

Common Use Cases

🔔 Quick Monitoring

  • */10 * * * * /scripts/check-api.sh - Check API status every 10 min
  • */10 * * * * /scripts/server-health.sh - Quick server health check

🔄 Fast Sync

  • */10 * * * * /scripts/sync-quick.sh - Quick data sync
  • */10 * * * * /scripts/pull-notifications.sh - Check for notifications

📊 Frequent Updates

  • */10 * * * * /scripts/update-prices.sh - Update pricing data
  • */10 * * * * /scripts/fetch-stock.sh - Fetch stock prices
