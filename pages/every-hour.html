Run Cron Every Hour

To run a job every hour at minute 0, use: 0 * * * *

Explanation

  • 0 = at minute 0
  • * = every hour (0-23)
  • * = every day of month
  • * = every month
  • * = every day of week

Hourly Variations

  • 0 * * * * - Every hour at minute 0
  • 30 * * * * - Every hour at minute 30
  • */2 * * * * - Every 2 hours
  • */4 * * * * - Every 4 hours
  • 0 */6 * * * - Every 6 hours
